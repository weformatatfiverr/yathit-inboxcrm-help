Yathit toolbar is shown below recipients input in Gmail compose and reply panels. Tick Archive checkbox in the toolbar to archive outbound email to SugarCRM.

Hover over to the Archive in the Yathit toolbar to see if information about your recipient is being displayed. If so, then a Lead or Contact record already exists in SugarCRM and youâ€™ll be able to save your email to their existing record. Otherwise, click the Details button to provide related records.
